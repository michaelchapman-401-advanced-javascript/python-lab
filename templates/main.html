About us:

At Salmon Cookies, we strive to bring you a delicious tasting cookie that is, in fact, shaped just like a salmon. We do this because we find the salmon to be the most majestic living creature on the face of this earth and our passions are uncontrollable! People call us crazy, but would crazy people introduce to the world cookies that represent the likeness and flavor of the most incredible creature to have ever lived? No... No they wouldn't...
